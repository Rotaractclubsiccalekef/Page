Rotaract

Rotaract is a service club that brings together young people from 18 to 31 years old. It was created in 1968 in the United States by Rotary International. Its name comes from the contraction of the terms ROTARy and ACTion.

Today there are over 7,500 Rotaract clubs in 156 countries with over 291,000 members.

What Will You Learn In Our Club

  • 1

    Becoming a peacemaker

    A Rotary Peacemaker Committee is a group of Rotarians who have come together to work towards furthering both the message and the cause of peace.

  • 2

    Becoming a volunteer in kef

    Volunteering is a great way to meet new people and practice social interaction. It does not matter whether you interact with one person or a group of people. It builds your social skills or builds your confidence around people.

  • 3

    Have fun

    Joining rotaract club is a great way to take part in new and fun events, take a break from studying and make the most of your university experience. The experience can help you to take on a new hobby, help with a cause close to your heart, find people with whom you share a common interest and have fun!.

  • 4

    Develop professional and leadership skills

    Rotaract helps to develop knowledge and understanding of the needs, problems, and opportunities in the community and worldwide.

  • 5

    Improve your personality

    It can give you a sense of achievement. Serving society is a wonderful thing to do and an achievement in itself. Additionally, it can take you outside of your comfort zone and give you an opportunity to face your fears. For example, if you fear social interaction or have social anxiety, sooner or later you will find a way to practice being around people.

  • 6

    Learn what is the meaning of life

    Discover that there’s more to university than assignments, tests, and a degree.Do what you love. Have the courage to follow your heart and intuition. They somehow already know what you truly want to become.

Who Should Attend Rotaract

Rotaract is a service club for young people ages 18 and up who are dedicated to finding innovative solutions to the world's most pressing challenges while developing leadership skills .
